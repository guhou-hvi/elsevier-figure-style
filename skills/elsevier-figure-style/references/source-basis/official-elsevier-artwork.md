## Operationalized Rules

- Check the current target-journal Guide for Authors before applying generic defaults.
- Prefer EPS/PDF for vector charts and TIFF/JPEG for appropriate bitmap artwork; verify the journal before using PNG or SVG as submission files.
- Use supported fonts where possible and embed them in vector exports.
- Keep normal final lettering around 7 pt and explicit text no smaller than 6 pt.
- Use 30/90/140/190 mm as generic minimum/single/1.5-column/double-width references when the journal supplies no override.
- Use 300/500/1000 dpi for halftone/combination/line-art bitmap exports.
- Prefer RGB for color artwork, avoid color-only meaning, and verify transparency handling.
- Keep captions separate or manuscript-level and ensure every artwork file is named, numbered, cited, and uploaded as instructed.
- Clear rights for third-party material and follow current generative-AI policy for graphical abstracts.
- Do not selectively enhance, obscure, move, remove, or introduce image features.
- For graphical abstracts, use a concise separate file at least 1328 x 531 px at 300 dpi with the 500:200 ratio, supported large fonts, minimal clutter, no unnecessary whitespace, and no `graphical abstract` heading.
