## Derived Strict-Profile Rules

- Keep figure formatting consistent across axis labels, legends, fonts, and language.
- Make captions contextual enough for readers to understand what the figure shows without hunting through body text.
- Keep panel label order and legend order aligned with visual panel order.
- Use clear legends, colorbars, and axis descriptions whenever colors, symbols, or matrices would otherwise be ambiguous.
- Keep labels visible, high-contrast, and consistently placed.
- Remove excessive software headers, decorative legends, or axis labels that do not help interpretation.
